Palm Reading

(Rating)

Palm reading, also known as palmistry or chiromancy, is a centuries-old practice that interprets the lines, shapes, and mounts on the palms of the hands to reveal insights about a person's character, personality traits, and potential future events.

Palmists examine various aspects of the hands, including the lines (such as the heart line, head line, and life line), the shapes and sizes of the palms and fingers, and the mounts (areas of the palm that correspond to different planets in astrology). Each of these features is believed to provide clues about different aspects of life, such as emotions, intellect, vitality, and career prospects.

Palm reading is often used as a tool for self-discovery and personal insight, offering individuals a deeper understanding of themselves and their life's journey. While interpretations can vary among practitioners and cultures, palmistry is commonly practiced worldwide as a means to gain guidance and perspective on relationships, health, finances, and overall life path.
